About this artist

I first discovered my love for music at 11. I was inspired mainly by Dashboard Confessional and the German band Tagtraum. Now I'm mainly into bands like Jimmy Eat World or Yellowcard. It all started in my room, I like to listen to music by myself especially the kind of music I really like with meaningful lyrics and heart-felt vocals. Some people might find my style a bit strange but I like to opening up my feelings and thoughts to others mainly through my music but also in other aspects of my style. I'm so proud and happy to have recorded my first song "So Sad". In my lyrics and my debut single I like to write about subjects like loneliness and searching for your place in the world. I would describe myself as an EMO girl and I crave two things: friendship and love!
